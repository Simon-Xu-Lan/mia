## 1.1 Number Sets

A **set 集合** is a collection of objects with a common property.
These objects are called the **elements 元素** of the set.

- **Natural Numbers** 自然数,  **$N = \set\{1, 2, 3, 4, . . .\}$**; positive whole numbers. 正的整数。
- **Integers** 整数, **$Z = \set\{0, ±1, ±2, ±3, . . .\}$**; positive and negative whole numbers including 0.
- **Rationals**, **$Q = \set\{ a \over b\}$**, where a, b are integers and $b \neq 0$;
- **Real Numbers**, **$R$**; including all of the above numbers.

## 2.1 Linear Equations 线性等式，线性方程式
* the word **linear** signifies that the highest power the variable (or unknown) is raised to is 1.
* **linear** 线性 是指未知数（一般用X表示）的最高指数是 1
* 求解线性方程式的方法
    * 1 Add/Subtract to both sides  两边同时加或减 一个数， 等式仍然成立。
  
      $x + 5 = 9$
        
      $x + 5 - 5 = 9 - 5$
      
      $x = 4$
      
      Tansposing, 上面的过程也可以是 Tranposing（把等式左边的项移到等式右边，改变该项的符号)
        
      $x + 5 = 9$
      
      $x = 9 - 5$
      
      $x = 4$
      
    * 2 Multiply/Divide both sides
      Divide both sides by 5
      
      $5x = 25$
      
      ${5x \over 5} = {25 \over 5}$
      
      $x = 5$
      
      Tansposing: 把左边的5移到右边， 相当于右边除以5
      
      $5x = 25$
      
      $x = {25 \over 5}$
      
      $x=5$
    * Combined Add/Subtract and Multiply/Divide, 同时用以上两种方法

## Index Laws 指数
- Defination: $a^n = a * a * a * a * ... * a$ (n个a相乘）
    - a is called the **base**  底数
    - n is called the **power, index or exponent** (指数，方）
    - The expression $a^n$ is read as the “**the nth power of a**”, or “**a raised to the power of n**”. **a的n次方**
- 2.2.1 Index Laws for positive indices.   指数运算规则（正指数）
    
    IL1. $a^m \* a^n = a^{m+n}$
    
    IL2. ${a^m \over a^n} = a^{m-n}$
    
    IL3. $(a^m)^n = (a^n)^m = a^{nm}$
    
    IL4. $(ab)^n = a^nb^n$
    
    IL5. $({a \over b})^n = {a^n \over b^n}$
    
- 2.2.2 Zero and Negative Indices

    IL6. $a^0 = 1$,   ($a \neq 0$)
    
    IL7. ${1 \over a^n} = a^{-n}$,  ($a \neq 0$)

## 2.3 Expanding and Factorising

**Expansion展开**: The process of multiplying all terms inside the brackets by the factor outside the brackets is called **Expansion**. 就是用括号外面的数或未知数或变量乘以括号内所有的元素。
    - 简单说， Expansion就是去括号
    - $a(b + c) = ab + ac$

**Factorisation因式分解**： 是Expansion展开反向操作。就是把common factors公因子提出来，变成common factors公因子乘以括号。 
    - 简单说， Factorisation就是变成带括号的乘积形式。
    - $ab + ac = a(b + c)$
    - a 是 ab 和 ac 的common factor公因子。 
    - $a(b + c)$ 有两个 factor因子， a 和 (b + c)

Example：
- Expansion:         $x * (2x+1) = x * 2x + x * 1 = 2x^2 + x$   
- Factorisation:     $3p^3 - 6 p = 3p * p^2 - 3p * 2 = 3p(p^2 -2)$

**cancel common factors from the numerator and denominator.** 抵消分子分母的common factor公因子

${4xy + 8y} \over {2xy^2 + 4y^2}$

$= {{2 * 2y(x + 2)} \over {2y^2(x + 2)}}$.   （x + 2) 和 2 是分子分母的common factor公因子，可以抵消

$= {2 \over y}$

Note: 只能抵消公因子common factor

### 2.3.2 Factorising Quadratic Expressions
A quadratic expression is defined as: 二次方程
$$ax^2 + bx = c$$
Notes:
- The highest power of x is 2. x的最高指数是2
- $a \nqe 0$. 如果 a = 0, 则方程式变成 一次线性方程。
- but b，c may equal 0.   b， c 可以为零。
- a, b and c are constants（常数） called coefficients（系数）.

### 2.3.3 The Discriminant(判别式）
Consider the quadratic expression: $ax^2 + bx + c, a \neq 0$. The discriminant 判别式 is denoted by
the Greek letter Delta ∆, and is defined as:
$$∆ = b^2 - 4ac$$
- $∆ > 0 =>$.  quadratic has 2 linear factors, 二次方有 2 个线性因子
- $∆ = 0 =>$.  quadratic has 1 repeated linear factors, 二次方有 1 个重复的线性因子
- $∆ < 0 =>$.  quadratic has no linear factors, 二次方程没有线性因子
