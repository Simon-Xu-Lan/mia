Busy Bee Florist

We are a small family-run local business, located and operating in Cairville for 5 years. We have, however, over 25 years of experience in floristry.

Our florist shop is located at 223 Bolsover St. We offer the exceptional quality and choice of fresh flowers & foliage tailor-made to suit any occasion.

We also do next-day home delivery (as long as the order is made by 3pm the day before).
